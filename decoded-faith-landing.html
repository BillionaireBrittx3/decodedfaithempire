John 3:16 - The World's Most Famous Verse

Common Translation:

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son..."

This implies God's love is about quantity or intensity

Original Greek Meaning:

"For God loved the world in this manner: by giving his unique Son..."

The Greek "houtos" indicates the method, not the intensity

The difference completely changes how we understand God's love and sacrifice.
